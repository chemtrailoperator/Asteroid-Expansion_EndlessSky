mission "Asteroid 0.1"
	outfitter
	invisible
	source
		outfits "Asteroid Scanner" "Research Laboratory" "Spectrometer Array" `"Tuur" Scanning Suite` "MCS Extractor"
	on offer
		conversation
			branch hasscanner
				or
					has "outfit (flagship installed): Asteroid Scanner"
					has "outfit (flagship installed): Research Laboratory"
					has "outfit (flagship installed): Spectrometer Array"
					has "outfit (flagship installed): Tuur Scanning Suite"
					has "outfit (flagship installed): MCS Extractor"
			`There is a buzz of conversation in the outfitter, a shop-owner beckons you over to a store selling asteroid scanners. "Can I interest you in a scanning system? There has been a breakthrough in scanning technology and our scanners now detect many more minerals and metals than before."`
			`	You thank him but say you aren't interested right now, although its probably something you should consider`
				accept
			label hasscanner
			`There is a buzz of conversation in the outfitter, a shop-owner beckons you over to a store selling asteroid scanners. "Since you already have one I thought I'd tell you. There's been a breakthrough in scanning technology and an update has been pushed to all scanners. Your scanner will now detect a much wider range of minerals at no extra cost."`
			`	You thank him for the info and move on.`
				accept
		log "Update pushed to all asteroid scanners from a breakthrough in scanning tech. Your scanner now detects a much wider range of minerals."
		
	on enter
		dialog `Your system now automatically detects many more minerals and metals than before. This scanning technology might be useful.`
		fail
