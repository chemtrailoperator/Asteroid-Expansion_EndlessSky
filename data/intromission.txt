mission "Asteroid 0.1"
	outfitter
	invisible
	name "Asteroid Scanner Update"
	description "Conversation flavor mission telling you that asteroid scanners have been updated."
	source
		government "Syndicate" "Republic" Free Worlds" "Pirate"
	to offer
		outfit (flagship installed): "Asteroid Scanner"
		not "Asteroid 0.2: offered"
	on offer
		conversation
			`	There is a buzz of conversation in the outfitter, a man beckons you over to a store selling asteroid scanners. "Since you already have one I thought I'd tell you. There's been a breakthrough in scanning technology and an update has been pushed to all scanners. Your scanner will now detect a much wider range of minerals at no extra cost."`
			`	You thank him for the info and move on.`
		log "Update pushed to all asteroid scanners from a breakthrough in scanning tech. Your scanner now detects a much wider range of minerals."
			accept
	on enter
		dialog `Your asteroid automatically detects many more minerals and metals than before.`
		fail
